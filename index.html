A little bit about me...

A quality of life leader, entrepreneur and friend, driven to improve lives and build healthy communities. A dual Canadian and USA citizen, I was raised from childhood in a Tibetan Buddhist monastery in India, after being recognized by His Holiness the Dalai Lama as the reincarnation of the renowned Buddhist master, the 22nd Gomo Tulku, from whom I adopted the name. During my 12 years in the monastery, I received extensive monastic training on cultivating the heart and mind.

I have traveled extensively, giving seminars on training the mind for optimal thinking, a journey of continuous learning, shared experiences and exploring connections with amazing people all over the world. My travels, along with my Buddhist education, has helped me shape a unique, worldly perspective on how we can approach our daily challenges and cultivate a meaningful life in this rapidly changing world.
